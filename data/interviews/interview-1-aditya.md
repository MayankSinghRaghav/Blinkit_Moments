# Interview 1 — Aditya

**29 · Software engineer · Bengaluru · orders 3–4×/week · segment: urban_professional**

**Q: Walk me through your last Blinkit order.**
I ordered chips, soft drinks, and ice cream because a few friends were coming over.

**Q: Did you browse before ordering?**
No. I searched directly because I wanted to place the order quickly.

**Q: Did you buy anything new?**
Not really. I bought the same chips and drinks I always buy.

**Q: Why not try something different?**
When people are coming over, I don't want to experiment. I know these products are safe.

**Q: If Blinkit suggested something for your party, would you consider it?**
Yes, if it explained why it's relevant. Like "People hosting cricket nights often buy this dip with nachos."

**Q: What stops you today?**
I don't know what goes well together. I don't want to waste money on something nobody likes.
