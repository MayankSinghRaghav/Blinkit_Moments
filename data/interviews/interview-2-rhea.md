# Interview 2 — Rhea

**26 · Marketing executive · Gurugram · orders daily · segment: power_user**

**Q: How do you usually shop on Blinkit?**
I already know exactly what I need before opening the app.

**Q: How often do you explore new categories?**
Very rarely.

**Q: Why?**
Because Blinkit is for speed. If I wanted to browse, I'd use Amazon or Instagram.

**Q: How do you discover new products?**
Mostly Instagram reels or influencers.

**Q: Would you like Blinkit to help you discover products?**
Only if it doesn't slow me down.

**Q: What kind of suggestions would be useful?**
Suggestions that match my current purchase instead of random recommendations.

---
**Key insight:** discovery must happen inside the existing flow, not as a destination.

**Reviewer note:** disconfirming evidence. "If I wanted to browse, I'd use Amazon or
Instagram" argues against in-app discovery as a category. Also sets a latency
constraint our LLM path (20s timeout) can violate.
